How To Play The Game

Tower of Hanoi consists of three columns or "towers" with a nunber of blocks placed one on top of the other inside each "tower".

The objective of the puzzle is to move the stack to another column following these simple rules.

Only one block can be moved at a time. No block can be placed on top of a smaller block.
